We are looking for innovators, changemakers, and leaders who want to get involved in their community and spread their passion for creativity and collaboration.

We are looking for committed teens in grades 8 to 12 who are willing to volunteer their time and effort this school year. Up to 35 volunteer hours can be earned and this experience also provides opportunities to network with peers and mentors.

eligibility.

+ You are in grades 8-12
+ You have technical skills if you are applying for App, Website, or Curriculum (Prior knowledge of coding skills or an interest to learn)
+ You have skills in writing, design, or public speaking if you are applying to Outreach, Business, or Media.
+ You are dedicated to continual communication over platforms like Email and Facebook
+ You have a genuine passion to help teach kids in your community about something you love!
